About Me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio page! My name is Sebastian Silvernagel and I am a 3D animator specializing in first person animations. I am currently looking for a full time permanent job as an animator. I mainly work in Blender but do have baseline experience animating, rigging and exporting files from Maya.

Ever since I was young I knew I wanted to be a part of the game development process, and until recently I didn't know it would be animation; and I couldn't be happier with my career path and I'm excited for the future.
